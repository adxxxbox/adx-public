# Epinephrine 8 mcg/mL  
in mcg/min Table

  
**Dose range  
For mcg/min**  
1-16mcg/min  
Administer at an initial rate of 1 mcg/min and titrate to effect  
At 20 mcg/min- pure alpha effects occur

* * *

**Dilute**  
2mg in 250ml NS= 8mcg/ml

* * *

**Desired Dose / 8mcg/ml x 60**

* * *

**Example:**  
you desire 2mcg/min= 2/8 x 60 =0.25 x 60= 15 ml/hr
