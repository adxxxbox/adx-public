# Esmolol (Brevibloc)Pharmacology and Notes

**Esmolol (Brevibloc) Quick Infusion Facts 34**

**Common Mix** Dilute 2.5 Grams in 250ml of NS or D5W= 10,000 mcg/ml (10mg/ml).

**Infusion Dose Range** 50-250 mcg/kg/min.  
Adjust infusion rate with increments of 25-50mcg/kg/min.

* * *

**For SVT, Intraoperative tachycardia, and/or hypertension.  
IV: Initial bolus:** 0.5-1 mg/kg over 1 minute, followed by a 150 mcg/kg/minute infusion.

**For control of postoperative hypertension**  
Titrate for desired heart rate and/or blood pressure up to 250-300 mcg/kg/minute if needed.  

* * *

**Method of Action:**Selective Beta 1 Blocker  
**Onset of action**: 2-10 minutes (quickest when loading doses are administered

* * *

  
**Half life:** 10 minutes.Duration of hemodynamic effects: 10-30 minutes, prolonged following higher cumulative doses, extended duration of use.  
**Half-life elimination:** Adults: 10 minutes, longer with Renal Disease

* * *

**Adverse reactions:**  
Severe Bradycardia  
Dose related hypotension  
Ventricular arrhythmias  
Heart failure

* * *

**Direct Contraindications:**  
Sinus bradycardia 1st degree heart block overt cardiac failure
