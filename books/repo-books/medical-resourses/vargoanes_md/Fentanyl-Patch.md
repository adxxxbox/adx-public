# Fentanyl Patch

**Fentanyl Patch**  
Used for long-term or chronic pain requiring continuous, around-the-clock narcotic pain relief.  
50, 75, and 100 mcg/hr patches  
Once a Fentanyl patch is applied, peak plasma levels are reached in around 18 hours.  
After the peak plasma levels are reached, blood concentrations will remain constant over the 72 hours that the patch is worn.  
When the patch is removed, it takes several hours with a GRADUAL decrease in plasma level, NOT IMMEDIATE- due to the ongoing absorption from the cutaneous depot.
