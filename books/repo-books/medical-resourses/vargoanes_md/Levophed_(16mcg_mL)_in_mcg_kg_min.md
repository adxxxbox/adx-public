<table><tbody><tr><td>4mg vial in 250mL bag NS or D5W = 16mcg/mL</td></tr><tr><td>8mg in 500mL bag NS or D5W = 16mcg/min</td></tr><tr><td>Doses as high as 0.5 to 1.5 mcg/kg/min for 1-10days have been used in septic shock.</td></tr></tbody></table>

  
**Equation:**  
Desired dose x kg x 60 / mcg/mL = mL per hr

* * *

**Example:**  
You desire 0.5mcg/kg/min for a 70k pt  
0.5 x 70 x 60 / 16 = mL/hr  
0.5 x 4,200/16=mL/hr  
2,100/16= 131.25  
so 131mL/hr = 0.5mck/kg/min
