### Complications Associated with Needle or Catheter Insertion

  
_Daniel D. Moos CRNA_

**Complications in this category include:**  
Inadequate anesthesia/analgesia  
Intravascular injection  
Total spinal anesthesia  
Subdural injection  
Backache (back pain)  
Post dural puncture headache  
Neurological injury  
Spinal or epidural hematoma  
Meningitis  
Arachnoiditis  
Epidural abscess
