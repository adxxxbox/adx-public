# Pulseless Arrest – Asystole and PEA

**Give epinephrine:**  
- IV/IO: 0.01 mg/kg of 1:10 000 (0.1 mL/kg)  
- ET: 0.1 mg/kg of 1:1000 (0.1 mL/kg)  
- Repeat: every 3–5 min  

**Give 5 cycles of CPR – recheck rhythm Asystole/PEA. Continue if no pulse. Give more epinephrine.**

Consider and treat possible causes:

| 5 Hs                        | 5 Ts                          |
|----------------------------|-------------------------------|
| Hypoxia                    | Tamponade                     |
| Hypovolemia                | Tension pneumothorax          |
| Hypothermia                | Toxins – poisons, drugs       |
| Hypoglycemia               | Thrombosis – coronary (AMI)   |
| Hypo / hyperkalemia        | Trauma                        |
| Hydrogen ion (**acidosis**) |                               |
