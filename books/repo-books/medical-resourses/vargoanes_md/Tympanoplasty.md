### Tympanoplasty

To repair a hole in the tympanic membrane.

An incision is made behind the ear and the ear is reflected forward. The edges of the perforated tympanic membrane are removed and a graft is placed under the membrane and the incision is then closed with absorbable sutures.

**Graft can be from:** Ear lobe, behind the ear or temporalis.  
**Anesthetic:** General/LMA or ETT (usually LMA). NO Nitrous!  
**Position:** Supine with operative ear up.  
**Duration:** 45-90 minutes  
**EBL:** Minimal  
Consider steroids and antiemitics.  
**Emergence:** Smooth
