### OB/GYN Abbreviations and Terms

**Multiparous:** A woman having borne more than one child  
**Primipara:** Giving or having given birth for the first time  
**Nulliparous:** A woman who has never given birth (same as Primipara or Primiparous)  
**Precipitous:** Labor to birth within 3 hours

**D&C:** Dilation and Curettage of the Uterus  
**EAB:** Elective Abortion  
**ECC:** Endocervical Curettage

**LPD:** Luteal Phase Defect

**PCO:** Polycystic Ovarian Disease  
**PID:** Pelvic Inflammatory Disease  
**PMS:** Premenstrual Syndrome  
**SAB:** Spontaneous Abortion  
**STD:** Sexually Transmitted Disease  
**SUI:** Stress Urinary Incontinence  
**TAH:** Total Abdominal Hysterectomy
