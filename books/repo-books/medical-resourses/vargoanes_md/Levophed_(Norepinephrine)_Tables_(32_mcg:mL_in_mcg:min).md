# Levophed (Norepinephrine)  
32mcg/mL in mcg/min

  
8mg vial in 250mL bag NS or D5W = 32mcg/mL

* * *

  
**Common infusion range:** 2-35 mcg/min  
**Popular maintenance:** 2-8 mcg/min  
**Refractory shock:** 80 to 250 mcg/minute (literature varies)  
**Single diluted bolus for intraoperative hypotension:** 2-4 mcg (diluted to 4 mcg/mL)

* * *

  
Desired Dose / 32mcg/ml x 60

* * *

**Example**  
you desire 2mcg/min= 2/32 x 60 = 0.0625 x 60= 3.75 ml/hr For Levophed
