**20mg in 100ml bag =200mcg/mL**  
**Equation**  
desired dose x kg x 60 / mcg/mL = mL per hr

* * *

**Example:**  
desired dose is 0.5mcg/kg/min on a 70kg pt  
0.5x 70 x 60 / 200= 10.5 mL/hr
