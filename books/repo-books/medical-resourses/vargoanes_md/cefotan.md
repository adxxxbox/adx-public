# Cefotan

**Cefotan (Cefotetan)**  
2nd Generation Cephalosporin (like Cefoxtin).  
A Cephamycin  
A Beta-Lactam Antibiotic, so avoid with beta lactam allergy.  
Penicillins and Cephalosporins are Beta-Lactams.  
Semisynthetic and broad-spectrum.  
**Doses**  
1-3 grams IV  
**Max:** 6 gams/day  
**Peds:**  
40-80mg/kg/day IV in divided doses Q12h  
**Max:** 6 grams/day  
Solution may be colorless or yellow.  
Aerobic Gram positive: weaker than 1st generation  
Aerobic Gram negative: Stronger than 1st generation  
**Indications**  
UTIs  
Lower Respiratory Tract Infections  
Skin and skin structure infections  
Gynecologic infections  
Intra-abdominal infections  
Bone and joint infections  
**Potential Side Effects**  
May increase serum creatinine  
**Surgery Prophylaxis** (Decreased incidence of infection)  
Abdominal or vaginal hysterectomy  
Tansurethral surgery  
Biliary tract surgery  
GI surgery
