| **Cardiac output** | Low | Low | HR dependent |
| **Stroke volume** | Low | Low | Fixed |
| **Contractility** | Reduced | High | Normal or reduced |
| **Anesthesia goals** |   | Minimize LVOTO if it is present |   |
| **Preload** | Maintain in the presence of ↑ LVEDP | Maintain/high | High |
| **HR** | Avoid tachycardia | 60-80 BPM | High (avoid bradycardia) |
| **Rhythm** | Sinus preferred | Maintain sinus rhythm | Sinus preferred |
| **Contractility** | Inotropic support often required.  
Ephedrine, levophed, dopamine,  
dobutamine, milrinone  
and epinephrine | Slight reduction is beneficial  
(avoid inotropes) | Promote with inotropes  
when required. |
| **Afterload** | Reduce or maintain, avoid increases  
Milrinone  
Nitroprusside  
Nicardipine (Cardene) | Increase slightly or maintain  
Phenylephrine | Maintain as cardiac output is fixed. |
| **Induction** | Ketamine or etomidate  
are recommended. | Blunt or avoid sympathetic  
outflow with direct laryngoscopy. | Ketamine is beneficial |
