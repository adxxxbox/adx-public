### Epidural - 8 cm Dilatation

**Epidural Started around 8 cm with rapid labor**  
  
At this stage, motor function must be preserved because the mother must start pushing soon. A CSE is often indicated at this point.  
  
**A Few Possible Doses:**  
20 mL or more of 0.125% Bupivacaine with 50mcg of Fentanyl  
10-12 mL of 0.25% bupivacaine with 50mcg of Fentanyl  
10-12 mL of 0.2% Ropivacaine  
Administer the boluses incrementally  
  
**Infusion** : (1) at 8-15 ml/h  
  
**Rescue Meds** :  
At this point, the patient is extremely uncomfortable or almost completely dilated.  
**  
Possible Top Off Doses**  
10 mL of 1% lidocaine  
10 mL of 2% 2-chloroprocaine
