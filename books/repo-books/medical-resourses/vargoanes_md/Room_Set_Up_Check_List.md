Warm room up. Ues a peds bair hugger (have warmer connected to warming blanket and already turned on) -if they are toddler or smaller in size.

Set your heart monitor on the Pediatric Mode, use 3 leads EKG system.

Have age-based OETT  plus next size larger and smaller.|

For predicted uncuffed OETT size = (Age / 4) + 4

Predicted Size Cuffed OETT size = (Age / 4) + 3

also 16 + Age (years) / 4 = ETT size

Also another method for predicted uncuffed OETT- subtract 0.5 if the patient< 10kg

Proper vent tubing, masks, flavored chap stick for the mask

ex uncuffed 4.0mm, so cuffed 3.5mm subtract 1.0 if kid > 10kg

Proper sized BP cuff –make sure the smaller cuff is compatible with the BP tubing from the monitor

Proper ventilator tubing, masks, and bag.

Small donut or rolled up blue towel for the back of the head

Tape for their eyes and endotube

Proper sized oral airways and some people also have nasal airways available
