# Inhalation Agents for CABG

**Gas Facts**  
  
Desflurane inductions have been demonstrated to cause pulmonary hypertension and myocardial ischemia.  
  
Desflurane is the only gas not recommended for CAD patients.  
  
Forane appears to be the most popular used in CABGs.
