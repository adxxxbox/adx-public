| Topical | 1.5-3.5 mg/kg, do not exceed 200 mg - literature varies |

Recommended dosage is up to 3 mg/kg (= 200 mg for the average adult; Katz and Lee, 1987).  
Some sources allow for use of even higher dosages (Henderson and Johns, 1977; Verlander and Johns, 1981).
