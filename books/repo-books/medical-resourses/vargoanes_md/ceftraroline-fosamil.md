# Ceftraroline Fosamil

**Ceftraroline Fosamil (Teflaro)**  
5th Generation Caphalosporin  
A new broad- spectrum antibiotic  
A Beta-Lactam Antibiotic, so avoid with beta lactam allergy.  
Penicillins and Cephalosporins are Beta-Lactams.  
**Dose:** 600mg IV  
Excellent activity against aerobic gram-positive cocci including MRSA.  
Works against Gram negative bugs in the same way as Rocephin and Claforan (3rd generation cephalosporins).  
Also effective against anaerobic gram-positive but not negative bugs.  
This drug is administered as the inactive prodrug ceftaroline fosamil, which is rapidly converted to ceftroline.  
**Indications:** complicated skin and skin structure infections (cSSSIs) and community- acquired pneumonia (CAP).  
Also potent against multidrug-resistant Streptococcus pneumonia.  
Semisynthetic and broad-spectrum.  
A Beta-Lactam Antibiotic, so avoid with beta lactam allergy.
