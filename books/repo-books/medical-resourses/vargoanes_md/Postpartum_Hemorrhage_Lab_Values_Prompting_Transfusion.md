### Postpartum Hemorrhage Lab Values Prompting Transfusion

| Blood product | Lab values  
prompting transfusion | Volume | Anticipated  
effect |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| PRBCs  
30-45 mins to crossmatch | Hct < 18 or  
Hct < 30 if unstable or  
active bleeding | 325 mL | Hct increases 3% per unit |
| Platelets (PLT)  
30-45 mins to thaw for release | < 50,000 or  
microvascular bleeding  
1:1 ratio with PRBCs | 50 mL | PLT count increases by  
7,500/mm3/U |
| FFP | INR > 2x normal  
1:1 ratio with PRBCs | 250 mL | Fibrinogen increases by  
10-15 mg/dL/U |
| Cryoprecipitate  
30-45 mins to thaw for release | Fibrinogen < 100 mg/dL | 40 mL | Fibrinogen increases by  
10-15 mg/dL/U |

Modified from “Characteristics of Blood Products, Anticipated Effects, and Complications”  
The American College of Association Obstetricians and Gynecologist
