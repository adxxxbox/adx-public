# Fenoldopam (Corlopam)  
40mcg/mL in mcg/kg/min Tables

  

<table><tbody><tr><td>10mg in 250mL NS =40mcg/mL</td></tr><tr><td>20mg in 500mL NS =40mcg/mL</td></tr></tbody></table>

  

No longer available in the US.

### Dosage

<table><tbody><tr><td><b>adult: </b>Hypertension: titrate to effect<br>initial 0.03-0.1 mcg/kg/min IV<br>Can increase every 15 min by 0.3 to 0.5 mcg/kg/min</td></tr><tr><td><b>Max Dose: </b>1.6 mcg/kg/min.<br>A bolus dose should not be used.</td></tr></tbody></table>

  
**Equation**  
Dilute 10 mg in 250ml NS= 40mcg/ml  
Desired Dose x 60 x kg / 40= ml/hr

* * *

**Example:**  
Wanting 0.1mcg/kg/min for a 40 kg patient  
0.1 x 60 x 40 / 40 = 0.1 x 2400 /40  
240 /40 = 6, so 0.1mcg/kg/hr = 6ml/hr
