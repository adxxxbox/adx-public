### Tubal Ligation-Laparoscopic or Mini-laparotomy

**Anesthetic:** General/ETT; for a spinal, which is rare:  You need a T4 sensory block.  
Either Bupivacaine or lidocaine are acceptable choices for the spinal.  
Hyperbaric 5% Lidocaine  1.4-1.6cc or hyperbaric 0.75% Bupivacaine 1.0-l.4cc seems to provide adequate analgesia for a sensory block to T4. Adding lipid soluble opioids, such as fentanyl, may improve the efficacy of smaller doses of Bupivacaine.  
**Duration:** 15-45 minutes  
**Position:** Supine  
**IV Access:** 20G is acceptable  
**EBL**: Minimal  
**Diagnosis**: Desired Fertility
