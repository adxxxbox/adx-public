### Preeclampsia vs Severe Preeclampsia Diagnosis

**Diagnosis of _Preeclampsia:_**  
Elevated BP ≥ 140 Systolic OR 90 Diastolic AND 1+ protein  
or elevated BP with > 300mg in 24 h urine  
minimal if any LFT elevation and no other problems.**  
  
Diagnosis of _Severe Preeclampsia:  
_**≥ 160 Sys OR 110 Diastolic on two separate occasions > 6h apart,  
Persistent 2+ or ≥ 3+ proteinuria  
\+ Scotomata (a partial loss of vision or blind spot in an otherwise normal visual field)  
RUQ/Mid epigastric pain  
Persistent frontal headache  
Oliguria < 30ml/h  
LFTs 2x elevated  
Platelets < 100K  
Elevated creatinine  
Pulmonary edema  
IU growth restriction  
Oligohydramnios
