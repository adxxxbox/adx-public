### LMA Contradictions and Complications

Lubricants containing Lidocaine are not recommended for use with the supraglottic airways.  
  
Lidocaine can delay the return of the patient’s protective reflexes expected before removal of the device, may provoke an allergic reaction, or may affect the surrounding structures, including the vocal cords.
