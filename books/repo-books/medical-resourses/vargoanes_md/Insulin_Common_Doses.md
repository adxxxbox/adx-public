# Insulin Doses

**For Hyperkalemia**  
5-10 units of IVP given with 25-50 grams of Dextrose 5%

**For Hyperglycemia**  
Load 0.1 unit/kg IVP  
Maintenance 0.1 units/kg/hr, titrate to desired glucose

Basic Operating Room Insulin Sliding Scale Glucose Levels Goal-Keep below 150  
<110 no action  
110-150 3 units repeat blood sugar in 1 hour  
150-200 6 units repeat blood sugar in 1 hour  
200-250 10 units repeat blood sugar in 1 hour  
\>250 20 units repeat blood sugar in 1 hour
