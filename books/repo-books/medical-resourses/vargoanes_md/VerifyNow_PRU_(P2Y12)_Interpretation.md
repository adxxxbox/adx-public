### VerifyNow PRU (P2Y12) Interpretation  
(Plavix Platelet Function Test)

**P2Y12 Test has reference ranges per package insert  
**180-376 PRU  
194-418 PRU (for P2Y12 inhibitor _naive_ patients)_**(Naive**_ no previous exposure)

| Result | PRU (P2Y12 Assay) |
| --- | --- |
| < 95 PRU | Hyper-responsive the clopidogrel. High risk for major bleeding. |
| < 180 PRU | Evidence of P2Y12 receptor blockade. |
| < 208 PRU | Appropriate response to anti-platelet therapy for cardiology patients. |
| \> 237 PRU | Hypo-responsive to clopidogrel 20% or less platelet inhibition due to P2Y12 receptor blockade. Associated with an increase of ischemic events after PCI including death, MI and stent thrombosis. |
| Not established | Optimal therapeutic and pre-surgical target. |
