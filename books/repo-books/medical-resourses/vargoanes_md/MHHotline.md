## MH Hotline (24 hours)

  
_Edited by Dan Lovinaria, DNP, MBA, MS, APRN, CRNA_

1-800-644-9737 (within the US)

00+1+209+417+3722 (outside the US)

The MH hotline should always be called ASAP with an MH crisis.

There are always three MH consultants available 24/7 via MH Hotline free of charge.  
They help healthcare professionals manage an MH crisis via per hotline.  
For liability issues, it highly recommended to consult with the experts from time of MH onset.

They will ask for your name, number, facility and  
email, in the event the call is dropped.

**AMRA** (Adverse Metabolic Reaction to Anesthesia) **Form**(Adverse Metabolic Reaction to Anesthesia) - not required, but should be filled out by anesthesia provider involved in the event. It can be filled out anonymously or with the patients consent with patient data. The data is entered onto the North American Malignant Hyperthermia Registry so the data can be used for research etc.
