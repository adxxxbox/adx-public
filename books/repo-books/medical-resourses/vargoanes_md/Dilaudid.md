# Dilaudid

**Dilaudid (Hydromorphone)**  
It interacts with mostly Mu and some Kappa receptors  
metabolized by the liver to the glucuronide conjugate which is then excreted in the urine.  
Dilaudid has been reported to be physically or chemically incompatible with solutions containing sodium bicarbonate and thiopental sodium.  
Adverse Effects- Nausea, vomiting, dizziness, somnolence, anorexia and constipation may occur
