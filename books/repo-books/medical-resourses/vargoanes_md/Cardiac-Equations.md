### Cardiac Equations

| Variable | Abbreviation | Formula | Normal Value |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Cardiac index | CI | CO / BSA | 2.8-4.21 |
| Stroke volume | SV | 1000 x (CO / HR) | 60-90 ml |
| Mean arterial pressure | MAP | Diastolic BP + 1/3 pulse pressure | 80-120mmHg |
| Systemic vascular resistance | SVR | (MAP-CVP) / CO x 80 | 900-1,400 dynes/sec/cm-5 |
| Systemic vascular resistance on pump | SVR | (MAP-CVP)/Pump Flow Rate x 80 | 900-1,400 dynes/sec/cm-5 |
| Pulmonary vascular resistance | PVR | (PAP-PCWP) / CO x 80 | 100-125 dynes/sec/cm-5 |

  
  
**Systolic Pulse Pressure (SPP)**  
SPP = Systolic – Diastolic  
Psystolic - Pdiastolic = 120mmHg - 80mmHg = 40mmHg  
  
**Pulmonary Pulse Pressure (PPP)**  
PPP = Pulmonary Systolic - Pulmonary Diastoilic  
Psystolic - Pdiastolic = 25mmHg - 10mmHg = 15mmHg
