# Cardene (Nicardipine) Dosages and Tables in mg/hour

**Doses:**  
40 mg of Cardene in a 200 mL bag = 200mcg (0.2mg)/mL  
50 mg of Cardene in a 250 mL bag = 200mcg (0.2mg)/mL

**Dose Range:** 5-15 mg/hour  
Maintenance dose: 5mg/mL

**Acute Hypertension**  
Many start out at 15mg/hour (because of a slow onset) and titrate down in increments of 5mg/hour.  
  
**Text Book:** Initially start at 5mg/hour and work up Q15 minutes in increments of 2.5mg/hour.

**Using a 250 mL bag of NS to get 200 mcg/mL (0.2 mg/mL) of Cardene):**

Use two (25 mg) vials (20 mLs total) of Cardene

\- Aspirate and waste 20 mLs from the 250 mL bag of NS

\- add 50 mg (20 mLs) of Cardene to the 230 mL bag- yields 200 mcg/mL (0.2 mg/mL)

  

**Using a 100 mL bag of NS to get 200 mcg/mL:**

Aspirate and waste 8 mLs from the 100 mL bag of NS

Add 20 mg (8 mLs) of Cardene to the 92 mL bag, yielding 200 mcg/mL (0.2 mg/mL)
