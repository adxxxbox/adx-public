### Stapedectomy

A surgical treatment for otosclerosis, a disease of ear bone degeneration that causes the stapes (a bone in the middle ear) to become fixed and prevents it from vibrating normally. During stapedectomy surgery, the stapes bone is removed (typically with a micro-laser), and a prosthesic of various materials is inserted in the middle ear as a replacement.

**Anesthetic**: General/ETT  
**Positioning:** Supine with the head turned away with ipsilateral arm tucked.  
**Muscle Relaxation:** Depends on facial nerve monitoring.  
**Duration:** 1-2.5 hours; depends on the surgeon.  
**IV Access:** 20G is acceptable  
**Facial Nerve Monitoring:** Check with the surgeon.  
**Emergence:** Smooth  
Consider PONV meds.  
**EBL:** minimal
