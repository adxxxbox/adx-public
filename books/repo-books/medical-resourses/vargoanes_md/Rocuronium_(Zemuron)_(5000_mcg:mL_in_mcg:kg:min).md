# Rocuronium (Zemuron) 5,000mcg/mL in mgc/kg/min

  
500 mg in 100ml NS = 5mg/mL (5,000mcg/mL)

* * *

**Initial Dose**  
600 to 1200 mcg/kg  
After you have twitches start your drip  
**Drip Rate**  
5-12 mcg/kg/min

* * *

**Equation below: (mcg/kg/min)**  
Desired dose x kg x 60/mcg/mL = mL per hr

* * *

**Example:**  
you desire 5mcg/kg/min for a 70kg pt  
5 x 70 x 60 / 5,000 = mL/hr  
21,000/5,000 = 4.2 so 4.2 mL/hr = 5mcg/kg/min
