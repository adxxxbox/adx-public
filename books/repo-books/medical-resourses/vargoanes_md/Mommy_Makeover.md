### Mommy Makeover

Many women notice changes in their bodies post-pregnancy and would like to obtain their pre-pregnancy figure. There are many areas of the body that can be addressed, most commonly a breast augmentation and abdominoplasty (tummy tuck).  
A mommy makeover is typically performed as a single stage procedure.  
  
**Possible procedures in a mommy makeover:**  
Breast augmentation  
Breast lift  
Buttock augmentation  
Liposuction  
Tummy tuck  
Vaginal rejuvenation  
  
**Anesthesia:** Usually general endotracheal.  
  
**Communicate with the plastic surgeon concerning:**  
Expected duration.  
Positioning.  
Whether muscle relaxation is preferred with abdominoplasty.  
Whether lidocaine is to be used with liposuction, and type of liposuction.  
  
Due to the prolonged duration, patient is usually intubated.  
If liposuction is done manage IV hydration cautiously.  
If the use of lidocaine-containing solution for liposuction is planned, systemic administration of lidocaine, as during induction, must be modified.
