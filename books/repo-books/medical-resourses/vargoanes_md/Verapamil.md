# Verapamil

**Verapamil (Calan)**  
**Class:** Calcium Channel Blocker.  
Rarely used anymore- so I will cover it minimally.  
**Indications:**  
SVT  
To temporarily control ventricular rate with A Fib/A Flutter.  
**Doses:**  
A Fib/A Flutter: 5-10mg ((0.075 to 0.15 mg/kg) over 2 minutes. and repeat in 30 mins.  
**Duration:** 30-60 minutes  
**Adverse Reactions:**  
Severe Bradycardia and/or Hypotension  
AV Block  
Severe  
CHF
