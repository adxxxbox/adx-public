### Myomectomy

A surgical procedure to remove uterine fibroids.  
**Anesthetic**: General/ETT  
**Duration:** 1-2 hours  
**Position:** Supine, sometimes lithotomy.  
**EBL:** Minimal to 200mLs  
**IV Access:** 20G is acceptable.  
**Complications:** Bleeding

Surgical Procedures and Anesthetic Implications; Lynn Fitzgerald Mackey, 2012.
