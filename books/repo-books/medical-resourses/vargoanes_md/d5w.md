# D5W

**D5W**  
(50 grams of dextrose in a liter bag of water).  
We rarely use it perioperatively.  
**Isotonic Solution:** Osmolarity about equal to serum, expands intravascular compartment.  
**Contains:**  
Dextrose Hydrous 50gm/L (260 mOsm/L)  
5% dextrose and water  
50g of dextrose per liter  
200 calories/liter  
Osmolality-280msmos/L  
**Classification**  
Isotonic then hypotonic  
Nonpyrogenic.  
Parenteral fluid  
Electrolyte  
Nutrient replenisher  
Provide a source of calorie replacement.  
The Dextrose is readily metabolized, may decrease losses of body protein and nitrogen, promotes glycogen deposition and decreases or prevents ketosis if sufficient doses are provided.  
**Indications**  
Is indicated as a source of water, electrolytes and calories or as an alkalinizing agent.  
**Contraindications**  
Solutions containing dextrose may be contraindicated in patients with known allergy to corn or corn products.  
**Use cautiously with:**  
Renal and cardiac patients.  
Can cause fluid overload.  
This is not really enough sugar to provide adequate energy, but may be beneficial to those patients who are diabetic and need a steady influx of sugar.
