### Epidural Top Off - Doses From Others

**Popular Doses  
  
Not ready to deliver  
**8-10 ml of the same local anesthetic you are using  
8-10 ml of 1% lidocaine  
8-10 ml of 2% 2-chloroprocaine  
Some practitioners will add 2 ml (100 mcg) of fentanyl.**  
  
Fully dilated - ready to deliver  
**3-5 ml of the same local anesthetic you have been using  
3-5 ml of 2% lidocaine  
  
**Other Top off Doses in literature  
**6 ml of 0.125% bupivacaine  
6 ml of 0.2% ropivacaine  
May repeat if needed  
  
**CRNA A  
Not ready to deliver:** 3-5 ml of 0.5% Marcaine (bupivacaine).  
**Fully dilated - ready to deliver:** Either 3-5 ml of 2% Lidocaine w/Epinephrine 1:200,000 or a combination of 3-5 ml of lidocaine w/0.25% Marcaine.  
  
**CRNA B  
Not ready to deliver:** 5 ml of 0.5% Marcaine (bupivacaine).**Fully dilated - ready to deliver:** 5ml of 2% lidocaine (bupivacaine).  
  
**CRNA C  
Not ready to deliver:** 5 ml of 1.5% Lidocaine with Epi. (Typical test dose vial) Effective, but hard to obtain.**Fully dilated - ready to deliver:** 2 ml of fentanyl  
  
**CRNA D**  
**Not ready to deliver:** 5-7 ml of 0.25% Marcaine (bupivacaine) with 2 ml of fentanyl**Fully dilated - ready to deliver:** 5 ml of 0.2% ropivacaine with 2 ml of fentanyl  
  
**CRNA E**  
**Not ready to deliver:** (3 possible doses)5ml of 2% Lidocaine  
5ml or 0.25% bupivacaine  
5 ml of 0.2% ropivacaine -all with or without fentanyl.  
  
If that is insufficient: 5 ml of 0.5% ropivacaine or 5ml of 0.5% bupivacaine.**Fully dilated - ready to deliver:** 3 ml of 2% lidocaine plus 2 ml of fentanyl  
  
**CRNA F**  
**Not ready to deliver:** 5 ml of 0.2% ropivacaine  
**Fully dilated - ready to deliver:** start with 1ml of fentanyl and add as needed.  
  
**CRNA G  
Not ready to deliver:** 5-10 ml of 0.5% ropivacaine and increase basal rate  
**Fully dilated - ready to deliver:** 10 ml of 2% lidocaine with 1:200,000 epinephrine  
  
**CRNA H**  
**Not ready to deliver:** 5 ml of 0.2% ropivacaine perhaps with 1-2 ml of Fentanyl  
**Fully dilated - ready to deliver:** (pushing within the next hours) 2 ml of fentanyl and perhaps 2-3 ml of 0.2% ropivacaine.  
  
**CRNA I  
Not ready to deliver:** 3-6 ml of 0.5% bupivacaine with or without 100 mcg fentanyl**Fully dilated - ready to deliver:** 5 ml of 2% chloroprocaine with or without 100 mcg fentanyl  
  
**CRNA J  
Not ready to deliver:** 5 ml of 0.25 bupivacaine + 2ml of fentanyl +3 ml NS (10 ml)**Fully dilated - ready to deliver:** 5-10ml of 2% lidocaine  
  
**CRNA K**  
**Not ready to deliver:** 5-10 ml from the bag infusing (0.2% ropivacaine + 2mcg/cc  
fentanyl).**Fully dilated - ready to deliver:** 5 ml of 2% lidocaine + 2 ml of fentanyl  
  
**CRNA L**  
**Not ready to deliver:** 5 ml .25% bupivacaine  
**Fully dilated - ready to deliver:** 3-5 ml 2% lidocaine
