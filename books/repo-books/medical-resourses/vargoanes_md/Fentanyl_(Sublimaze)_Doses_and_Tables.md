# Fentanyl (Sublimaze)  
50mcg/mL in mcg/kg/hr

  
Does not have to dilute.  
**Normal Drip Range:**1-3 mcg/kg/hr  
A prolonged effect of fentanyl may be seen after infusion is discontinued due to tissue acculumlation and then re-entry to plasma.

* * *

  
**Equation** mcg/kg/hr = desired dose x Kg/ concentration

* * *

**Example:**  
Say you want 2mcg/kg/hr on a 75Kg patient  
2 x 75 / 50 = mL/hr  
150/ 50 = 3 mL/hr  
so 2mcg/Kg/hr is 3mL/hr
