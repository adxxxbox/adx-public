### Succinylcholine (Anectine, Quelicin) Infusion Table

**1mg/ml at mg/min  
Adult Infusion dose range:** 0.5-10 mg/min.  
**Common dose range:** 2.5-4.4 mg/min.  
  
Dilute 20mg/ml down to 1mg/mL.  
**1mg:1mL:** (remove 14.5 mLs from a 250 mL bag of 0.9% NS or D5W and add 14.5 mLs of succinylcholine; 250mg).  
mg/min x 60 = ml/hr (for a 1:1 solution).
