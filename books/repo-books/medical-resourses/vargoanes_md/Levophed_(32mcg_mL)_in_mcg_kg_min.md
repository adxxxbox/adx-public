**Equation:**  
Desired dose x kg x 60 / mcg/mL = mL per hr

* * *

**Example:**  
You desire 0.5mcg/kg/min for a 70k pt  
0.5 x 70 x 60 / 32 = mL/hr  
0.5 x 4,200/32=mL/hr  
2,100/16= 65.625  
so 66 mL/hr = 0.5mck/kg/min
