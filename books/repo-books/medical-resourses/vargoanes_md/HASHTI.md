### HASHTI

An acronym referring to treatment of anticoagulated bleeding patients.  
For emergent bleeding – implement HASHTI (see below).  
  
**H**old further doses of anticoagulant.  
**A**ntidote – consider if one exists.  
**S**upportive treatment (volume resuscitation and hemodynamic support–inotropes and monitoring).  
**H**emostatic measures (local or surgical)–consider antifibrinlytics; Amicar or tranexamic acid).  
**T**ransfusion.

1.  Red blood cells for severe or symptomatic anemia.
2.  Platelets if thrombocytopenia (<50 x 109 /L) or patient on long-acting antiplatelet agents.

**I**nvestigate for bleeding source.
