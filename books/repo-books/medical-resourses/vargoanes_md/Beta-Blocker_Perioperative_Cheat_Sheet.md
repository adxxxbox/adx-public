| **Labetalol**  
(Trandate)  
Selective A1 and  
Nonselective B1, B2. | 1-5 minutes  
4-8 hours  
1-4 hours | 5-20 mg | Beta to alpha potency ratio: 7:1 (IV).  
May repeat doses of 20-80 mg IV Q 10  
minutes until desired BP is achieved.  
Caution with COPD due to B2 antagonism.  
Reported perioperative bronchospasms  
in COPD patients following labetalol  
are extremely rare. |
