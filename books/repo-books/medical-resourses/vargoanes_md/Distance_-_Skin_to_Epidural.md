### Distance - Skin to Epidural

**Average distance from the skin to epidural space  
Average is 4cm                              
  
Thin adult:** 3 cm  
**Average adult:** 4-6 cm  
**Obese adult:** up to 8 cm     

**![](avgDistEpi.png)**

  
In thin individuals, the more superficial location of the epidural space must be kept in mind when advancing an epidural needle.    

Chestnut, David.(2014) Chestnut’s Obstetric Anesthesia Principles and Practice.
