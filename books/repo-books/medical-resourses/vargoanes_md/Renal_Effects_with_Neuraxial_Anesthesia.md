### Renal Effects with Neuraxial Anesthesia

Neuraxial blockade has little effect on the blood flow to the renal system. Autoregulation maintains adequate blood flow to the kidneys as long as perfusion pressure is maintained.  
Urinary retention with neuraxial anesthesia is independent of the GFR.
