# Amrinone(Inocor) DoseTables  
(150mg ampl)

  
**Loading Dose**  
0.75 mg/kg (in 2-3 min)  
Bolus doses can be administered undiluted.  
Repeat once in 30 minutes.

* * *

**Maintenance Dose**  
5-10 mcg/kg/min

* * *

**Dilute**  
200 mg in 250 mL bag of NaCl= 0.8 mg/mL (800mcg/mL)

* * *

**Equation**  
desired dose x 60 x kg / mcg/mL

* * *

**Example:**  
Say you want 5mcg/kg/min on a 70kg pt  
5 x 60 x 70 / 800 = mL/hr drip rate  
\= 21000 / 800= 26 mL/hr So 26 mL/hr is 5mcg/kg/min
