### Crani Room Set-Up

ASA monitors  
Vent extension tubing; stretch it out.  
A line kit and set up: have it zeroed.  
**Table Top:**  
Etomidate or Propofol  
Have extra syringe of Propofol for Mayfield Pins  
Muscle Relaxants and reversals ready  
Esmolol for induction

ETT #7 and 8 with stylet; check the ETT cuff.  
Neo and Nitroglycerine primed in a micro-drip tubing.  
Neo and Ephedrine Syringe  
Nitroglycerine diluted syringe: Draw up NSS 3cc and 1cc of NTG.  
If the Nitro bottle is 200mcg/cc, once 1cc of NTG is mixed with 3cc of NS in your syringe, the concentration should be 50 mcgs/cc.     
Lasix  
Decadron –patient is usually already on steroids.

Mannitol 25%  
Dilantin- rarely used now. Give it 20mg/min (1 gram/hr) on a pump. If you push it too fast, it can cause a decrease in BP, seizures. It also attenuates neuromuscular blockade so  check your train of four twitches or Double Burst Stimulation often.

Blood warmer ready with Blood tubing  
Remifentanil or Sufenta drip if your plan is to give an inhalational gas at 0.5 MAC.
