### Weight Gain Changes with Pregnancy

Weight gain varies from person to person and can be anywhere from 5 pounds to 100 pounds (45 kg).  
In America, the physician recommended weight gain range is 25 to 35 pounds.  
Mean maternal weight gain with pregnancy is 12kg or 17% from pre-pregnancy weight.
