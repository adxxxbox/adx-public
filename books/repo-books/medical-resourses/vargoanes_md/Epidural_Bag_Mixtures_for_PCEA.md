### Epidural Bag Mixtures for PCEA

There are many different PCEA bag mixtures between institutions and are tailored per the anesthesia department requests. They are mixed by the pharmacy department. It is almost always a mixture with a low concentration of local anesthetic and diluted narcotic.  
Lower concentrations of local anesthetics are analgesic with reduced motor blocking properties.
