# Heparin  
In Units/Hour

**Dosage**  
Typical loading dose: 5,000 IU (International Units) IV, someone  
resistant to heparin may need a larger dose

* * *

**Maintenance dose**  
Infusion therapy is typically started at 800 -1,000  
IU/hour. This dosage is modified based upon the patient's PTT or  
ACT
