### During Full Bypass, Relax and Prepare

There is not much to do but prepare for coming off bypass. Prepare for following case if applicable.  
  
Protamine: have accessible, but separate from other drugs.  
Place all other meds neatly on your tabletop.  
MgSO4 to be given when unclamping the aorta.  
CaCl2 1 gram.  
  
Epinephrine: diluted syringe.  
Fentanyl: For re-dosing.  
Benzodiazepine: Re-dose prior to re-warming.  
Muscle relaxant for re-dosing.  
  
Have inotrope ready.  
  
Have albumin or colloid such as hetastarch hanging and primed.  
Get blood products checked and ready if their use is anticipated.  
Prepare for the next case if applicable.  
  
**Remain aware of:**  
What surgeon is doing.  
Whether the perfusionist is giving blood.  
Current labs.
