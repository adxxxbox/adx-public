### Epidural Dosing of Levobuvacaine

Levobuvacaine is available in the US  
  
**Levobupivacaine is:  
**Long acting  
An S isomer of bupivacaine (racemic mixture)  
Used in the same concentrations as bupivacaine  
Clinically acts just like bupivacaine with the exception that it is less cardiotoxic
