# Intra Lipid Drip

**Treatment for Local Anesthetic-Induced Toxicity**

1.5mL/kg of 20% Intralipid - slow IV Push (1 min) Quickly follow with drip rate of 0.25mL/kg/min until pt is revived (continue chest compressions for circulation). Repeat bolus every 3-5 mins up to 3mL/kg until pt is revived. Can increase drip rate to 0.5mL/kg /min if BP declines. Max Dose of 8mL/kg is recommended

**Example:** 70kg patient  
Grab 500mL of 20% Intralipids and a 60 or 50mL syringe.  
Draw out 50mL x 2 and push 100mL over 1 min.  
Spike Intralipid bag with IV drip tubing and run it at 0.25mL/kg/min.  
Can give 2 more 100 mL boluses (up to 3mL/kg) is pt is not revived.  
Refer to: www.lipidrescue.org
