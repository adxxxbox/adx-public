### Alternate Anticoagulants for HIT Patients

Bivalirudin (Angiomax)  
Argatroban (Acova)  
Fondaparinux (Arixtra)  
Lepirudin (Refludan, recombinant hirudin)  
Desirudin (Revask, Iprivask, Hirudin)  
Danaparoid - Not available in the US  
  
The American College of Cardiologists (ACC) recommend using **Bivalirudin** or **Argatroban** in patients who have had, or at risk for, heparin induced thrombocytopenia (HIT) and are undergoing percutaneous coronary intervention.
