# Precedex  
(Dexmedetomidine)  
Infusion Notes and Tables

  
200 mcg/2 mL (100 mcg/mL) in a glass vial-always dilute to 4mcg/mL  
aspirate 2 mLs from a 50mL bag of NS, add ONE 2mL vial to it to total 50mLs = (4mcg/mL)  
Aspirate 4 mLs from a 100mL NS bag, inject 2 vials = (4mcg/mL)  
Aspirate 10mLs from a 250mL NS bag and inject 5 vials (10mLs -1,000mcg) = 4mcg/mL  
**Dose Range 0.2-1.0mcg/kg/hr**

* * *

**mcg/kg/hr = desired dose x kg/ concentration**

* * *

**Example:**  
say you want 0.4mcg/kg/hr on a 40Kg patient  
0.4 x 40 / 4 = mL/hr  
16/ 4 = 4 mL/hr So 0.4mcg/Kg/hr 4mL/hr

* * *

**Warning-Fast bolus may result in hypotension, widened QRS and sudden arrest**  
ICU-Bolus 1mcg/kg over 10 minutes, followed by a maintenance infusion of 0.2 to 0.7 mcg/kg/hr.  
  
Anesthesia- Bolus 1mcg/kg over 10 minutes, followed by a maintenance infusion of 0.4-0.7mcg/kg/hr.

* * *

**Tip**  
If the patient weighs 70 kg, drip rate of 7mL/hr will =0.4mcg/kg/hr  
85kg, drip rate 8.5mL/hr gives you 0.4cg/kg/hr  
(some skip the bolus, start the drip early at 0.4mcg/kg/hr) For Precedex 4mcg/mL
