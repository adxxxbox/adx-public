### Heparin In Units/Hour

**Dosage**  
Typical loading dose: 5,000 IU (international units) IV, heparin-resistant patients may need a larger dose.  
  
**Maintenance dose**  
Infusion therapy is typically started at 800 -1,000 IU/hour.
