### Intravascular Volume

Increased NO production and decreased sensitivity to NE and Angiotensin II  
(Pregnancy induces a vasodilated state that mimics HYPOvolemia)  
     **↓**Activation of the Renin-Angiotensin-Aldosterone System  
(Volume retention and expansion)
