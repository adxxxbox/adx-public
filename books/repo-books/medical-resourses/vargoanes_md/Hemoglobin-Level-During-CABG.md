### Hemoglobin Goals During CABG

The Hemoglobin ALWAYS drops due to hemodilution and blood loss.  
  
**Hemodilution (Rationale for modest fluid restriction).** 1L crystalloid causes a 17% reduction in Hb.  
1L of crystalloid decreases the Hb from 12g/dL to 10g/dL.  
The patient will receive at least 1 Liter of crystalloid from a primed CPB machine.  
Crystalloid from anesthesia 1-2 Liters.

The standard goal is to keep the Hb above 7.5g/dl prior to terminating cardiopulmonary bypass.
