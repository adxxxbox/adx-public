If timing does not allow for gradual reduction of INR from  
withholding warfarin alone, consider antidotes.  
  
**Doses with INR  
**The doses of Warfarin are adjusted  
to maintain a target INR of 2.5  
(INR range, 2.0-3.0) for all treatment durations.
