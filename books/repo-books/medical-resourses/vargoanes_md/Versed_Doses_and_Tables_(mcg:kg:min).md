# Versed (Midalazam) Doses and Table

  
Loading Dose: 2-5 mg or 25-100 ug/kg IVP  
50mg in 50mL of NS or D5W=1mg/mL (1,000mcg/mL)

* * *

**Equation below: (mcg/kg/min)**  
Desired dose x kg x 60 / mcg/mL = mL per hr

* * *

**Example:**  
you desire 0.5mcg/kg/min for a 70k pt  
0.5 x 70 x 60 / 1,000= mL/hr  
2,100 / 1,000 =2.1 so 2.1mL/hr = 0.5mcg/kg/min
