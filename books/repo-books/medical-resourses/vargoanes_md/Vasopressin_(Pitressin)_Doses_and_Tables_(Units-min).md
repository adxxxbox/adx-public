### Vasopressin (Pitressin) Doses and Tables (Units/min)

Mix 100 units in 250 ml NS or D5W= 0.4 units/mL  
Mix 200 units in 500 bag = 0.4 units/mL  
Have NTG hooked up and ready to counteract if needed.  
  
**Septic Shock** .01-.04 units/min  
  
**Anesthesia - most popular:** 0.02 or 0.04 units/min for hypotension in the operating room.  
**Dose IV Infusion Range** 0.01-0.1 units/min
