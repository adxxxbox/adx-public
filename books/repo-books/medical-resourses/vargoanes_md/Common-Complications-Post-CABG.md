### Common Complications Post CABG

Stroke: 2% - 4%  
Delirium: 8% - 15%  
Atrial Fibrillation: Up to 35%  
Renal Failure: 1%  
Mediastinitis (Chest Wound Infection): 1-2%  
Temporary Mild Neuropsychiatric Effects: 90% (multiple air emboli)  
Death: 1-3% (Depends on risk)
