### Membranectomy-Eye

Part of an eye procedure.  
This is the removal of layers of unhealthy tissue from the retina with minute instruments such as forceps (tiny grasping tools), picks (miniature hooks), and Visco-dissection (separating layers or tissue with jets of fluid).
