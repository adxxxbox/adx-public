### Hemabate (Carboprost)

**Class:** Oxytocic  
A synthetic prostaglandin analog of PGF2a with oxytocic properties.  
**MOA:** It mimics the action of the naturally occurring prostaglandin F2 alpha by stimulating smooth muscle and uterine contractions.  
  
**Standard dose:** 250 mcg IM (NOT IV) Q15 to 90 minutes  
  
**Main indication:** Refractory postpartum bleeding  
**Other indication:** Induce abortion between 13-20 weeks of pregnancy for the expulsion of the products of conception.  
It may be given later for other reasons.  
  
**Common intraoperative indications:  
**Post-cesarian section for postpartum bleeding.  
D&C (Dilation and Curettage) procedure for bleeding.  
D&E (Dilations and Evacuations) procedure for bleeding.**Use caution with asthmatics:** May cause severe bronchospasm**Pharmacokinetics:  
Peak plasma time:** 20-30 minutes  
**Half-life:** 3 hours  
**Protein bound:** None  
**Excretion:** Urine
