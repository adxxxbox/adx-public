### Precedex (Dexmedetomidine)

4 mcg/cc - Loading Dose is 0.5 mcg/kg x 30 minutes  
  
**Note:** Most infusion pumps can not be set over 30 minutes.  
Doubling the calculated VTBI and enter it as the hour infusion rate, will deliver the correct VTBI over 30 minutes.  
  
**Ex.** To deliver 20 mcg (5 mLs) over 30 minutes:  
Set hourly rate to 10 mL/hour.  
Set VTBI to 5 mLs.
