### Synovectomy

Usually part of an orthopedic arthroscopic procedure. Half the time you are not aware the procedure was performed until the surgeon states it was done at the end of the case

**Synovectomy:** To remove partial or all the synovial membrane of a joint. The synovium lines the joint.

**Indication:** Treatment of inflammation and excessive growth of the synovium. 

Boezaart, André P. _Anesthesia and Orthopaedic Surgery_. New York: McGraw-Hill Medical Pub. Division, 2006.
