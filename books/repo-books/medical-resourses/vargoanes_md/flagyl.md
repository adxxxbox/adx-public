# Flagyl

**Flagyl (Metronidazole)**  
5-nitroimidazole  
Antibacterial & antiprotozoal antibiotic  
**Doses**  
500 mg over 30-60 minutes  
Does not need redosed  
Give prior to incision  
Very effective against nearly all anaerobic gram positive bacteria.  
One of the few antibiotics effective against C. difficile (C-diff).  
**Common Surgery uses:**  
Prostate biopsy  
Vaginal surgery, including urethral sling procedures  
involving entry of urinary or GI tract.  
Flagyl has been associated with Propofol syndrome
