### ASA Physical Status Classification System

A scoring system assesses and communicates a patient’s pre-anesthesia medical co-morbidities.  
Established by the American Association of Anesthesiology (ASA).  
  
**ASA score range:** I-VI  
The lower the score, the healthier the patient.  
  
When the ASA status is used with other factors (e.g., type of surgery, frailty, level of deconditioning), it can help predict perioperative risks.

**COPD Severity Criteria by Spirometry:  
As a general rule, spirometry that becomes normal after a bronchodilator is not COPD!**  
The spirometric criterion for diagnosing COPD is a ratio below 0.7 AFTER bronchodilator.
