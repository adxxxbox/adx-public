### ICP Values

Normal ICP: 5 - 15 mmHg  (Supine).  
Mean ICP should remain < 15mmHg.  
Term Infant ICP: 5-6 mmHg.  
Children ICP: 3-7mmHg.  
  
**ICP of 20mmHg:** is the extreme upper normal limit.  
**ICP >20-25mmHg**:can cause loss of pressure autoregulation.  
**ICP >25-30mmHg**: can compromise CPP and CBF, even with a normal MAP.  
Sustained levels can cause severe neurologic injury.  
**ICP>40mmHg:** Mortality rate is 65%.  
**ICP>60mmHg:** Mortality rate is 100%.

**ICP > 15mmHg in infants** should be treated.  
**ICP >18 mmHg in children** < 8 years should be treated.  
**ICP >20 mmHg in older children and teenagers** should be treated.
