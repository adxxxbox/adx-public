### Eyelid Laceration- Repair

Repair of a torn eyelid. This usually occurs from a trauma. The laceration repair goal is for lid function to be restored to the integrity of the lid. The defects are classiﬁed according to size and location. The major causes of the eyelid defect are trauma and tumors; basal cell carcinoma is the second most common causes.

**Anesthetic:** General or MAC with a field block.  
**Duration:** Depends on the tear.  
**EBL:** Minimal  
**IV Access:** 22G is acceptable.  
**Anesthetic:** General/usually LMA. A 500mL NS bag with micro drip tubing is popular.  
**Position:** Supine with arms tucked.
