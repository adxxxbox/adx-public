# Dantrolene Sodium (Dantrium) Treatment and Prophylactic Dose  

  
**Treatment** **For MH**  
2.5 mg/kg IV every 5 minutes, through large bore IV if possible, until symptoms subside.  
May require a total dose up to 30 mg/kg  
Dantrolene vial comes in a lyophilized powder of 20 mg/bottle  
Use a 60 mL syringe and mix with 60 mLs of sterile WATER  
**Remember:** each 60 mL syringe only contains 20 mg of Dantrolene.  

* * *

**Prophylactic IV**  
2.5 mg/kg, starting approximately 30-60 mins before anticipated anesthesia and infused over approximately 1 hour.  
  
When patient can tolerate po may substitute 1-2 mg/kg po qid.

If refractory (NMS) consider adding bromocriptine 5 mg tid initially -if response is inadequate, increase dose rapidly to max of 10-20 mg po q6h. D/C when patient improves or CPK (normal).

  
Ensure good IV (large vein) or central line because it burns!

* * *

**Preperation**  
Reconstitute powder for IV infusion in 60 mL of sterile water without bacteriostatic agent.  
Shake until solution is clear.  
Store at room temperature for up to 6 hr. Protect from direct light.  
Use 60mL syringe and large bore needle
