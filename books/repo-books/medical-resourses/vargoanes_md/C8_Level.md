### C8 Level (little finger)

**_Daniel D. Moos CRNA_**

The C8 Level is where you DO NOT want to reach.  
It is too high. Most likely you have blocked the cardio-accelerator fibers (T1-T4), the patient becomes severely hypotensive and may arrest.
