# Isoflurane

**Isoflurane (Forane)**  
Halogenated methyl ethyl ether  
The slowest and cheapest Inhalation agent we use.  
**Color Code:** Purple  
**Blood:** Gas Partition Coefficient:1.46.  
**MAC (30-55yo):** 1.17%  
**MAC (> 60yo):** 1.0  
**Peds MAC:** 0.8-1.2  
**Vapor Pressure:** 240  
**Prevents Recall:** 0.4 MAC  
0.5-1 MAC decreases amplitude and increases latency of somatosensory evoked potentials.  
High blood solubility so it has a slow onset. The slowest one we use.  
Nonflammable liquid at room temperature.  
Has pungent, ethereal odor.  
**Degrades to CO with Soda Lime:** Yes  
Slight airway irritant  
The cheapest Inhalation Agent  
Suppresses convulsive activities: Yes
