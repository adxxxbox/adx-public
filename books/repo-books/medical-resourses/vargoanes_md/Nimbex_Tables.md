# NIMBEX (cisatracurium besylate)

**Doses and Tables**  
Comes in 5 mL, 10 mL, and 20 mL vials  
Dilute 40mg in 200mL bag of NS (200mcg/mL)  
Intubation Dose: 0.1-0.2 mg/kg  
Loading Dose- 0.1mg/kg  
Maintenance Dose Range - 0.5-10.2 mcg/kg.min  
Usual drip rate- 3mcg/kg/min (usually adequate)  
  
Dilute 40mg in 200mL bag of NS (200mcg/mL)

* * *

**Equation (mcg/kg/min)**  
Desired dose x kg x 60 / mcg/mL = mL per hr

* * *

**Example**  
Say you desire 2mcg/kg/min for a 70k pt  
2 x 70 x 60 / 200= mL/hr  
8400 / 200 = 42 so 42mL/hr = 2mcg/kg/min
