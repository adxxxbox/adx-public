### Celiac Reflex

Celiac reflex is the onset of bradycardia, hypotension, and apnea resulting from traction or pressure on structures within abdominal and thoracic cavities.  
  
**Nerve involved:** From vagus nerve (cranial nerve # 10) stimulation.
