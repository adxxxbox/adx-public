### Lidocaine-Ketamine Infusion

(based on ideal body weight)\- A **lidocaine** bolus of 1.5 mg/kg within 10 minutes preoperatively- diluted in 100 mL of saline.  
\- Then, continuously infuse lidocaine intraoperatively at 3 mg/kg/hour.  
\- A bolus dose of ketamine of 0.35 mg/kg should be 10 minutes preoperatively diluted in 100 mL of saline.  
\- Followingly, continuously infuse **ketamine** intraoperatively at 0.2 mg/kg/hour (diluted in a 60 mL saline) and at a rate of 20 mL/hour.  
A 60 mL syringe can be used with a syringe pump.
