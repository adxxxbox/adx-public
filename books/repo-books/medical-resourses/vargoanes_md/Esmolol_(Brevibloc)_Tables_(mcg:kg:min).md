# Esmolol (Brevibloc)  
10mg/mL in mcg/kg/min

  
2.5 Grams in 250ml of NS or D5W= 10,000 mcg/ml (10mg/ml)

* * *

  
**Equation**  
desired dose x kg x 60 kg / 10,000

* * *

**Example:**  
you want 50mcg/kg/min for a 40kg pt=  
50 x 40 x 60 / 10,000= 120,000 / 10,000 = 12ml/hr

* * *

**Infusion Dose Range 50-250 mcg/kg/min**  
Adjust infusion rate with increments of 25-50mcg/kg/min

* * *

  
For SVT, Intraoperative tachycardia, and/or hypertension  
IV: Initial bolus: 0.5-1 mg/kg over 1 minute, followed by a  
150 mcg/kg/minute infusion.  
For control of postoperative hypertension  
Titrate for desired heart rate and/or blood pressure up to 250-300  
mcg/kg/minute if needed.
