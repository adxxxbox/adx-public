### Warfarin non-urgent reversal prior to elective surgery

Stop warfarin 5 days prior to procedure.  
  
Check INR 1-2 days prior.  
  
If INR >1, administer Vitamin K 1.25- 2.5 mg PO.  
  
For supra-therapeutic INR, consider Vitamin K 5-10 mg PO.  
  
Consult cardiology if patient has mechanical heart valve (high risk of thrombosis).
