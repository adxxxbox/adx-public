### Kcentra Dosing for Reversal of Warfarin

FDA approved Kcentra doses for reversing Vitamin K antagonist due to acute, major bleeding or an urgent surgical procedure.

| INR | Dose of Kcentra | Max Kcentra Dose |
| --- | --- | --- |
| INR: 2-4 | 25 IU/kg | 2,500 IU |
| INR: 4-6 | 35 IU/kg | 3,500 IU |
| INR: > 6 | 50 IU/kg | 5,000 IU |

**Infusion Rate:** 0.12 ml/kg/minute up to the maximum infusion rate of 8.4 ml/minute.  
  
Co-administer vitamin K when reversing a vitamin K antagonist prior to an urgent surgical procedure.
