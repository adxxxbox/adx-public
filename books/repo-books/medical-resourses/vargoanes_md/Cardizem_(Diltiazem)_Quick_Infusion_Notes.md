# Cardizem (Diltiazem) Quick Infusion Notes and Dose Table

  
**For Rapid Afib  
**20 mg bolus (undiluted) .  
**THEN  
**

Infusion rate: 5-15 mg/hr

Just mix in 1mg/ml ratio (usually 100mg/100ml) in IV bag of NS or D5W

**For Paroxysmal Supraventricular Tachycardia (PSVT)  
**20mg (0.25mg/kg) dose given over 2 minutes (slow push)  

If no conversion to normal sinus rhythm

**THEN**

25mg (0.35mg/kg) may be given 15 minutes after the initial dose

**For A-Fib/Flutter**

20mg (0.25mg/kg) dose given over 2 minutes

If no conversion

**THEN**

25mg (0.35mg/kg) may be given 15 minutes after the initial dose.

If continued reduction in ventricular rate is required, initiate infusion at 10mg/hr.

Infusion rate may be increased by increments of 5mg/hr up to 15mg/hr. Maximum is 15mg/hr
