### Transport the Patient to OR

Recheck infusion bags, ensuring they are all full (fluid restriction is minimized at this point).  
  
Before transporting the patient to the OR, check with the circulating RN concerning:  
  
Proper signatures on consent(s) and history and physical.  
  
Availability of perfusionist.  
  
Availability of blood.  
  
Presence of surgeon in the hospital.  
  
Necessity for surgeon to see patient preoperatively.  
  
Transport patient carefully, protecting lines. Ensure availability of help.  
  
Organize lines before transferring the patient to the operating table.  
  
Slide the patient on to the operating room table.  
Organize lines and connect monitors.
