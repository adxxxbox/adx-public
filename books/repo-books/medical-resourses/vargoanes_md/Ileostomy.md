### Ileostomy

When the small intestine is attached to the abdominal wall in order to bypass the large intestine. These are sometimes part of the end of a GI resection. Sometimes they are done alone.

**Anesthetic:** General/ETT  
**Muscle relaxation:** yes  
**Nitrous Oxide:** No  
**Duration:** 1 hour  
**A-Line:** not indicated  
**IV Access:** 20G is acceptable.  
**Position:** Supine   
**EBL**: 10-50 mLs.
