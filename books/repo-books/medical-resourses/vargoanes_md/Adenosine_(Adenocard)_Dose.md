# Adenosine (Adenocard) Dose  

  
Have 20cc flush syringes available  
**Dosage**  
6mg IVP peripherally  
IF unsuccessful, may repeat with 12mg IVP peripherally or 6mg IVP  
centrally to a max 30mg. Each dose should be followed by 20cc of saline flush  
Always have connected to a cardioverter just incase

* * *

**Routes**  
IV; should be administered directly into a vein or into the  
medication administration port closest to the patient and followed by  
flushing of the line with IV fluid.

* * *

**Pediatric Dosage**  
Safety in children has not been established.
