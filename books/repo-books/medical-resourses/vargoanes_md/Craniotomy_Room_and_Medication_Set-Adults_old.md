## Craniotomy Room Set-Up

**_Edited by Chrissy Salts, CRNA_**

**Monitors and Equipment:**  
\- Standard monitors  
\- Arterial line kit ready and transducer zeroed  
\- Blood warmer and tubing ready  
\- Appropriate size pediatric ETT with stylet, check the ETT cuff  
\- Ventilator circuit will be stretched out for table to be turned  
\- Suction  
  
**Medications: Verify weight-based dose of each medication for the pediatric patient.**  
\- Mask induction for pediatric patents without and IV  
\- IV Induction with or Propofol and Fentanyl for pediatric patients with an IV  
\- Extra syringe of Propofol available for Mayfield Pin insertion  
\- Muscle Relaxants and Reversal Agents  
\- Epi, Neo and Ephedrine syringes available  
\- Steroids- patient may already be on steroids so stress dose will be required  
\- Mannitol 25% available, verify with surgeon if Mannitol will be given  
\- Remifentanyl or Sufenta drip if plan is inhalational anesthetic at 0.5 MAC  
\- Dilantin - rarely used now  
      - verify weight based pediatric dose for the patient  
      - rapid administration can cause decrease blood pressure and seizure  
      - attenuates neuromuscular blockade so check train of four and double burst stimulation often
