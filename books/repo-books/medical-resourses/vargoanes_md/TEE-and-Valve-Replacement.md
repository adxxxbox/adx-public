### TEE and Valve Replacement

TEE has become the standard of care during a valve replacement.  
It can further refine pre-op diagnosis very specifically whether it’s stenosis or regurgitation.  
TEE during pre-bypass has changed perioperative plans up to 19% of the time.  
Used to identify air when removing air from the heart.

For surgical results 5-18% of the time, the TEE reveals findings that result in a returning to CPB.

Cheng, Davy C. H., and Tirone E. David. _Perioperative Care in Cardiac Anesthesia and Surgery_. Philadelphia: Lippincott Williams & Wilkins, 2006.
