# Dobutamine (Dobutrex)

### 500mg/250ml Bag (2,000mcg/ml)

  
**Equation**  
Desired Dose x kg x 60 / 2,000 = ml/hr drip rate

* * *

**Example:**  
order of 5mcg/kg/min for a 70 kg pt=  
(5 x 70) x 60= 21,000/2,000  
\= 10.5 ml per hr for 5mcg/kg/min

* * *

For Dobutamine(Dobutrex) 2,000/mcg/ml
