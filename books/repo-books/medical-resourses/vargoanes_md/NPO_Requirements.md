### NPO Requirements

ASA fasting recommendations for healthy patients who are undergoing elective procedure requiring general anesthesia, regional anesthesia, or procedural sedation and analgesia.  
Not intended for parturients in labor.

| Ingested material | Minimum fasting period | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Clear liquids | 2 h | Water, fruit juices without pulp, soft drinks, carbonated beverages, clear tea, and black coffee without cream |
| Breast milk | 4h |   |
| Infant formula | 6h |   |
| Nonhuman milk | 6h | Nonhuman milk is similar to solids in gastric emptying time |
| Light meal | 6h | Toast and clear liquids |
| Fried foods, fatty foods, or meat | 8 or more hours | Meals that include fried or fatty foods or meat may prolong gastric emptying time. |
