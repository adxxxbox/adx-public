# Neosynephrine (Phenylephrine) 40mcg/mL  
in mcg/kg/min

  
One 10mg/vial in 250mL of NS or D5W = 40mcg/mL

* * *

**Equation (mcg/kg/min)**  
Desired dose x kg x 60 / mcg/mL = mL per hr

* * *

**Common dose:** 0.5 mcg/kg/min, titrate to effect.

* * *

**Example**  
you desire 0.5mcg/kg/min for a 70k pt  
0.5 x 70 x 60 / 40 = mL/hr  
2100 / 40 = 52.5 so 53 mL/hr = 0.5mcg/kg/min

* * *

For 2100 / 80 = 26.25 so 53 mL/hr = 0.5mcg/kg/min
