# Cardene (Nicardipine) Dosages and Tables in mg/hour

**Cardene (Nicardipine)  
**0.1mg/mL in mg/hour  
  
25 mg of Cardene in a 250 mL bag = 100mcg (0.1mg)/mL  
20 mg of Cardene in a 200 mL bag = 100mcg (0.1mg)/mL

**Dose Range  
**5-15 mg/hour  
Maintenance dose: 5mg/mL

**Acute Hypertension**

Many start out at 15mg/hour and titrate down in increments of 5mg/hour.

**Text Book:** Initially start at 5mg/hour and work up Q15 minutes n increments of  
2.5mg/hour.

**Using a 250 mL bag of NS to get 100 mcg/mL (0.1 mg/mL) of Cardene:** Standard

\-Aspirate and waste 10 mLs from the 250 mL bag of NS
