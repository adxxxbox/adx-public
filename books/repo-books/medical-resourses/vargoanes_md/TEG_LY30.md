**TEG LY30**

  

**LY30** 0-8%  
Basically shows clotting stability and clot breakdown.  
LY30 is the percentage of amplitude reduction 30 minutes after its maximum amplitude(MA).  
LY30 is a measure of Fibrinolysis.
