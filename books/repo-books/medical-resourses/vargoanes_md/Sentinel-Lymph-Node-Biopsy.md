### Sentinel Lymph Node (SLN) Biopsy

Sentinel node biopsy is a surgical procedure used to determine if the breast Cancer has spread beyond a primary tumor into the lymphatic system, thus becoming a metastatic process. The sentinel nodes are the first few lymph nodes into which a tumor drains.
