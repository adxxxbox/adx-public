### LMA Contradictions and Complications

**Contradictions:**  
\- Patients with a risk of gastric aspiration (non-fasted, Gastro Esophageal Reflux Disease, hiatal hernia)\- Patients with airway morbidities (Respiratory tract infections, COPD, etc.)\- Restricted mouth opening (< 2.5 cm)\- Distorted airway anatomy and airway obstruction\- Prolonged duration of surgery (> 2 hours)\- Surgery involving the upper airway\- Maxillo facial trauma\- Morbidly obese patients  
**  
Possible complications:  
**\- Regurgitation and aspiration\- Misplacement of mask and airway obstruction\- Malposition or dislodgement of LMA\- Upper airway trauma\- Inadequate sealing of airway and leaks\- Cough and laryngospasm\- Gastric insufflation\- Vocal cord palsy and nerve injuries (Lingual nerve, Recurrent Laryngeal Nerve, Hypoglossal Nerve, Glossopharyngeal Nerve)
