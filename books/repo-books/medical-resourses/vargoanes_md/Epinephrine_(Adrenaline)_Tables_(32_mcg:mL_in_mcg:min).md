# Epinephrine 32 mcg/mL  
in mcg/min Table

  
**Dose range  
For mcg/min**  
1-16mcg/min  
Administer at an initial rate of 1 mcg/min and titrate to effect  
At 20 mcg/min- pure alpha effects occur

* * *

**Dilute**  
8mg in 250ml NS= 32mcg/ml

* * *

**Desired Dose / 32mcg/ml x 60 = mL/hr

* * *

**Example:**  
you desire 2mcg/min= 2/32 x 60 =0.0625 x 60= 3.8 ml/hr
