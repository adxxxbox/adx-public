### Sickle Cell and Cardiac Surgery

Maintain normal temperature.  
Ensure adequate hydration.  
  
Position with caution to avoid or minimize stasis of blood.  
  
Patients with sickle cell trait or anemia may tolerate hypothermia, but normothermia is nonetheless recommended.
