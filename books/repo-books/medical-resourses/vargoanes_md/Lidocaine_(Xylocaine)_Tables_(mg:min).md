# Lidocaine(Xylocaine) Doses and Table mg/min

  
2 grams in 500mL Bag of NS or D5W = 4mg/mL  
2 grams in 250mL Bag of NS or D5W = 8mg/mL

* * *

**Loading Dose:**  
1.0-1.5 mg/kg IVP and repeat 0.5-1.5 mg/kg IVP q 3-5 min until arrhythmia is controlled or to a Max of 3mg/kg

* * *

**Maintenance Dose**  
2-4 mg/min

* * *

**VF/VT**  
Begin a continuous infusion at 2-4mg/min  
Use 2-2.5 times the IV dose when given via ET tube; followed by 10 ml saline flush
