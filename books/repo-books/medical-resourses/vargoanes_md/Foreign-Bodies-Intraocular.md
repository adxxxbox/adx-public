### Foreign Bodies- Intraocular

The size, shape, direction and velocity of impact of the foreign body, as well as its path through the eye, affect the final outcome. Some of these patients have had CT scans done first.  
**Anesthetic:** General with ETT  
**Duration:** 30-60 minutes  
**EBL:** Minimal  
**IV Access:** 22G is acceptable  
**Position:** Supine and arms tucked
