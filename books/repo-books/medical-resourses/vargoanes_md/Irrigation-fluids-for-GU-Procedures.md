### Irrigation fluids for GU Procedures

The irrigation fluids vary with procedures and urologists.  
They usually use Level 1 tubing to warm it.  

**Bipolar NaCl:** is popular for Turps  
**NaCl:** Is popular for Ureteroscopy.  
**Water:** Bladder Biopsies.  
**Glycine:** Turp with Monopolar

Glycine and Sorbitol: Are well documented but rarely used anymore.

The circulator and surgeon always monitor I & Os of the irrigating fluid.
