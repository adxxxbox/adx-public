### Cystectomy (Partial)

A partial removal of the bladder. When the cancerous lesion is localized to the dome of the bladder, a urinary diversion is NOT needed.  
**Etiology:** Contracted bladder, hemorrhagic and radiation cystitis.  
**Anesthetic:** General/ETT  
If Spinal-T4 sensory level.   
**Duration:** Only 2 hours, because a urinary diversion is not needed.  
**Position:** Supine  
**EBL:** Minimal  
**Frequency** of Occurrence Male : Female 3:1  
**Type and Cross:** Not needed.  
**A-line:** Usually not  
**IV Access:** 20G is acceptable  
  
**Possible Complications**  
Bleeding  
UTI  
Wound infection  
Reduction of bladder capacity  
Urinary extravasation  
Ileus
