### Salpingo-oophorectomy (SO)

The removal of a SINGLE fallopian tube and the ovary at the same time. This is usually done with a hysterectomy.  
See _Hysterectomy._  
  
BSO is removing both- Bilateral Salpingo-Oophorectomy
