# Cefepime

**Cefepime (Maxipime)**  
4th Generation Cephalosporin  
Very similar to 3rd generation cephalosporin  
Semisynthetic and broad-spectrum  
A Beta-Lactam Antibiotic, so avoid with beta lactam allergy.  
Penicillins and Cephalosporins are Beta-Lactams.  
**Doses:** 1-2 grams IV  
**Peds:** 2mo-16years, <40kg in wt-  
50mg/kg IV Q8-12h  
**Advantage over 3rd Generation Cephalosporins:**  
More rapid penetration through the outer membranes of many gram negative bugs.  
For more information: see Rocephin (it's sister drug).
