# Versed (Midalazam) Pharmacology and Notes

**Class:** benzodiazepine

* * *

**Indications:** ICU sedation, pre op sedative hypnotic, anxiolytic acute anxiety states  

* * *

**Half-life**_:_ 1-4 hours

* * *

**Metabolism**_:_ liver, excreted in urine, some in feces

* * *

**Dosing Frequency**_:_ Q1-2 hours, to continuous drip

* * *

**Precautions**_:_ respiratory depression- more common with versed then the other Benzos, especially when used concomitantly with narcotics

* * *

**Side Effects:**  
Drowsiness  
hypotension  
amnesia  
respiratory depression  
apnea.

* * *

**Adverse Effects**  
Transient Hypertension  
Cimetidine can prolong half life

  

* * *

**Contraindications:**  
Patients with known versed hypersensitivity  
narrow-angle glaucoma  
shock

* * *

**Antidote:**Flumazenil (Romazicon)
