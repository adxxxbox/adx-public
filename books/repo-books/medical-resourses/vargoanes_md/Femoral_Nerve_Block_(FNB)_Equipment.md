### Femoral Nerve Block (FNB) Equipment

**The recommended equipment to perform an FNB:  
**\- Sterile gloves\- Ultrasound machine with linear transducer (8â€“18 MHz)  
\- Ultrasound gel  
\- Sterile sleeve (optional for a single shot)  
\- Standard nerve block tray  
\- Peripheral nerve stimulator (optional)  
\- A 50- to 100-mm, 22-gauge, short-bevel needle or 18-gauge Touhy needle with a catheter.  
\- Insulated stimulating needle (when using nerve stimulator)  
  
**A 20 mL syringe of one of the following local anesthetics:**  
0.5% Bupivacaine or  
0.5% Levobupivacaine or  
0.5% Ropivacaine  
  
**For FNB with a catheter for post-op continuous infusion:**  
\- All of the above  
\- Sterile drape  
\- Sterile sleeve  
\- Continuous nerve catheter  
\- Tegaderm to secure the catheter
