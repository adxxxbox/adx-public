# Diflucan

**Diflucan (Fluconazole)**  
**Class:** Antifungal agent  
**Doses:** (premixed)  
200 mg (100mL bag)  
400 mg (200 ml bag)  
**Indications**  
Vaginal Candidiasis  
Oral Thrush (Oropharyngeal Candidiasis)  
Esophageal Candidiasis.  
Systemic Candida infections  
Candida urinary tract infections and peritonitis  
Cryptococcal meningitis  
Prophylaxis in patients undergoing bone marrow transplant.  
**Side effects:**  
Nausea, upper stomach pain, itching, loss of appetite, dark urine, clay-colored stools, jaundice fever, chills, body aches, flu symptoms.  
Severe blistering, peeling, and red skin rash;  
easy bruising or bleeding, unusual weakness; or  
seizure (convulsions).
