### Epidural Dosing of Etidacaine (Duranest)

Long acting amide local anesthetic  
Rarely used in OB due to the profound motor blockade it induces.  
When used for surgical anesthesia it is used in a concentration of 1%.  
Epinephrine works very little with etidocaine and other long lasting local anesthetics.
