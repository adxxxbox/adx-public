### Chondroplasty

Usually part of an orthopedic arthroscopic procedure. Half the time you are not aware the procedure was performed until the surgeon states it was done at the end of the case.

Debridement/shaving of articular cartilage. Cutting out damaged cartilage and reshaping or smoothing out the underlying healthy tissue, allowing healthy cartilage to grow in its place.
