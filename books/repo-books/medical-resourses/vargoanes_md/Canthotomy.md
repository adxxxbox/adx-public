### Canthotomy

Incision of a canthus. This procedure is needed to perform a Canthorrhaphy. It may be performed prior to cataract extraction or in conjunction with other procedures. It may also be done to relieve an elevated intraorbital pressure following orbital trauma.  
**Anesthetic:** General, ETT if trauma has occurred or if the globe is exposed.  
**Duration:** 30-60 minutes.  
**EBL:** Minimal  
**IV Access:** 22G is acceptable. A 500mL NS bag with micro drip tubing is popular.  
**Position:** Supine with arms tucked.
