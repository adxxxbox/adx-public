## Post Laryngospasm Care

Confirm a clear airway  
Exclude/rule out Negative Pressure Pulmonary Edema  
CXR if suspecting pulmonary edema  
Explain and reassure the patient  
Observe for 2-3 hours  
Humidified O2 to decrease laryngeal irritation

Gavel, G., & Walker, R. W. M. (2014). Laryngospasm in anesthesia. _Continuing Education in Anaesthesia, Critical Care & Pain_, 14, 2, 47-51.
