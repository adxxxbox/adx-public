### Cranial Nerves -12

1\. **Olfactory** (sensory)-smell

2\. **Optic** (sensory)-vision in each eye

3\. **Oculomotor** (motor)-pupil contraction + eye movement

4\. **Trochlear** (motor)-turns the eye to look down, laterally, and medially.

5\. **Trigeminal** (motor and sensory) motor: the ability to clench jaw

    Sensory: facial response to touch

6\. **Abducens** (motor) lateral deviation of eye

7\. **Facial** (motor and sensory)

      Motor: ability to smile, frown, lift eyebrows.

      Sensory: taste sensor for anterior 2/3 of the tongue.

8\. **Acoustic** (sensory): hearing

9\. **Glossopharyngeal** (motor and sensory)

    Motor: swallow and gag reflex

    Sensory: taste posterior 1/3 of the tongue

    Also sensory innervation to carotid bodies for carotid blood pressure.

10. **Vagus** (motor and sensory)

       Motor: swallow

       Sensory: ask the patient to speak

       Also sensory innervation to receptors in aorta for aortic blood pressure.

11\. **Spinal Accessory** (motor):shrug shoulders against resistance; controls swallowing.

12\. **Hypoglossal** (motor): controls tongue movements.
